About Galaxy Screens

Galaxy Screens is equipped with 4 state-of-the-art screens, each named after Telugu cinema icons and offering a premium movie experience. Here are the screen names and seating capacities:

  • Ramoji Screen: 90 Seats
  • NTR Screen: 70 Seats
  • Mahesh Babu Screen: 40 Seats
  • Vijay Deverakonda Screen: 80 Seats
